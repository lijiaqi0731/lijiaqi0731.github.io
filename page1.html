CAR MODIFICATION

A car modification is classed as any change made to a vehicle that enhances it in some way and isn’t part of the original manufacturer’s specification. People mostly make modifications to cars to improve their aesthetics or performance.The most common modifications made to cars include louder exhausts, tinted windows, neon lights, and enhanced sound systems.With many cars, it’s obvious to tell that they’ve been upgraded but with others it isn’t, so be careful if you buy a second-hand vehicle, as it can invalidate your insurance if you fail to disclose a modification on your policy.
